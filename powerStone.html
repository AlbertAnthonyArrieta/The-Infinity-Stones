DESCRIPTION:

The Power Stone represented the destructive nature of the universe, and was incredibly powerful, capable of annihilating entire planets. The purple stone first appeared in the first Guardians of the Galaxy movie and had a major role. Star-Lord, a junker stole it from the planet Morag to sell it for some credits. It was later found in the hands of Ronan to use it for his evil doings but was defeated in a dance battle against Star Lord. The Guadians then gave it to the Nova Corps in the planet Xandar to protect the stone from being taken again. Eventually, Thanos and his army retrieves the stone off camera in the movies and destroyed Xandar using the stone before the events of Avengers: Infinity War.
