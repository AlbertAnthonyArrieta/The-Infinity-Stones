SUMMARY

At the end of Infinity War, Thanos had successfully collected all the infinity stones and in doing so, using all the powers of the infinity stones all at once, he was able to exterminate half of all life in the universe with the snap of his fingers. On Earth, the majority of world governments were in a state of chaos because half of the world's population had disappeared. Over the course of five years, the victims of the Snap would come to be known as the Vanished, with memorial sites erected in their honor in every city in the world.

In 2018, after Thanos had achieved his lifetime goal of killing half of all life in the universe, via the power of the Infinity Stones, he used the Space Stone to teleport himself to the Garden, where he settled at his farm, while smiling contentedly at the sunrise. A few weeks after, Thanos used the stones to detroy themselves in order to avoid being tempted to use their power for personal gain, though this ended up wounding him badly and scarring him due to the gamma radiaation. It also damages the infinity gauntlet and resulted in the gauntlet being fused to his hand.
