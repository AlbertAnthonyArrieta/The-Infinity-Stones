DESCRIPTION:

the Reality Stone is described as having the ability to warp reality to the wielder's will, allowing them to manipulate matter and energy, alter the physical properties of objects, and create illusions. The first appearance of the stone comes from Thor: The Dark World as the “Aether” used by Malekith. After the events of Thor: The Dark World, the Asgardians gave it to The Collector in Knowhere to ensure it’s safekeeping away from someone just like Malekith. Eventually Thanos finds his way to Knowhere to retrieve the stone in Avengers: Infinity War.
