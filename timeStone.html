DESCRIPTION:

The time stone holds dominion over the forces of time. The green infinity stone first appeared in the first Doctor Strange movie, portrayed as a necklace worn by Doctor Strange and was used later to fight Dormamu. After the events of Doctor Strange, Stephen Strange became the sworn protector of the stone until Avengers: Infinity War where he gave it away to save the life of Tony Stark on the planet Titan. The stone was later used by Thanos to retrieve the Mind Stone from Vision.
